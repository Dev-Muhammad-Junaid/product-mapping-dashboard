Bulk Ingredient Mapping

Map multiple instances of the same ingredient across different products at once. This is useful for ingredients that appear frequently across your product catalog.

How Bulk Mapping Works

Ingredients are grouped by their normalized names. When you map one group, all instances of that ingredient across all products will be mapped to the same target ingredient.
